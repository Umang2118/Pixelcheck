Fighting AI Fraud in Food Delivery

PixelCheck was built to protect platforms like Swiggy and Zomato from fraudulent refund claims using AI-generated food images

The Problem We Solve

After a food order is delivered, a growing number of dishonest customers photograph their meal, feed it to an AI image generator like DALL-E or Stable Diffusion, and create a convincing fake—showing stale food, insects, or other quality issues. They then submit this AI-generated image as "evidence" for a refund claim.

Because human support agents can rarely distinguish AI fakes from real photos, platforms lose millions in illegitimate refunds every year. PixelCheck changes that equation permanently.

Our deep learning model—built on Microsoft's CvT vision transformer—is trained to detect the subtle pixel-level artifacts that AI generators leave behind. Integrating with our API takes minutes, and every claim image is classified in under 2 seconds.

Our Capabilities

  • Detect images generated by DALL-E, Midjourney, Gemini, Chatgpt, Stable Diffusion & more
  • Classify food quality fraud: fake bugs, mold, staleness artefacts
  • REST API integration for Swiggy, Zomato & any delivery platform
  • Under 2-second response time per image
  • 99.2% accuracy on validation datasets
  • No image data stored — complete privacy guarantee
  • Confidence scores to guide human reviewers
