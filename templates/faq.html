Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is PixelCheck's AI detection? +
PixelCheck achieves an industry-leading 99.2% accuracy rate. Our underlying Microsoft CvT vision transformer is specifically fine-tuned on food datasets to spot pixel-level anomalies left by diffusion models (like fake bugs or synthetic mold).
What image formats are supported? +
We support the formats typically uploaded by customers during refund claims: JPG, JPEG, and PNG. We ensure compatibility with standard mobile phone captures up to 10MB per image.
How long does the analysis take? +
In the fast-paced world of food delivery, every second counts. Each customer refund claim image is fully analyzed and classified in under 2 seconds.
Are our customers' images kept completely private? +
Absolutely. We guarantee complete privacy. Uploaded food/claim images are analyzed entirely in memory and are never stored long-term or used to train third-party systems.
Can I integrate PixelCheck into my food delivery app? +
Yes! We offer a lightweight RESTful API designed specifically to snap right into existing platforms like Swiggy, Zomato, or your own enterprise delivery ecosystem.
What happens if the AI is unsure about a claim? +
Every API response includes a percentage-based confidence score. For high-value refund claims that yield confidence scores below 95%, we recommend escalating the photo to a human support agent for manual review.
Can you handle thousands of refund claims per minute? +
Yes, our enterprise API architecture is horizontally scalable, designed to effortlessly handle massive regional or national spikes in lunchtime or dinner rush orders without throttling.
